# Vellera.app 90-Day Marketing Roadmap

## Phase 1: Validation & Beta (Days 1-30)
**Goal**: Establish product-market fit with EDS inner circle + measure referral loop

### Week 1-2: Internal Beta
- Onboard Asaad + Shauntze as founding coaches
- Deploy Referral Portal, ProgressCard, Coach Showcase to production
- Enable Coach Showcase for both founders: `/coach/asaad` and `/coach/shauntze`
- Internal referral campaign: aim for 5-10 beta student signups

### Week 3-4: Viral Hook Validation
- Test ProgressCard shares on Instagram Stories (manual tracking)
- Monitor referral activation rate (target: >40% convert after 3 drills)
- Collect testimonials from beta users
- Measure: viral coefficient (K = activated referrals / total referred)

**KPIs**:
- 20 beta signups via referral
- 40%+ activation rate (complete 3 drills)
- 2-3 multi-generational referral chains

---

## Phase 2: Micro-Influencer Launch (Days 31-60)
**Goal**: Acquire first cohort via niche influencers + SEO momentum

### Week 5-6: Influencer Outreach
**Target**: 10-15 micro-influencers (5K-50K followers)
- BJJ/MMA coaches on Instagram & TikTok
- Fitness coaches in tactical/conditioning space
- Approach: "Free premium lifetime access + revenue share on student referrals"
- Provide: Coach Showcase URLs + unique referral codes

### Week 7-8: Content Seed
- Create 5 "Coach Spotlight" landing pages (EDS-360 integration):
  `/coaching/martial-arts/asaad`
  `/coaching/tactical-fitness/shauntze`
- Deploy structured data (Schema.org) on all coach pages
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- Seed blog posts:
  - "Best Training Feedback Apps for Coaches" (link: coach showcase)
  - "Why Video Analysis is Revolutionizing BJJ"

**KPIs**:
- 50 coach signups
- 500 student invites via influencer links
- 20-30% activation rate from paid traffic

---

## Phase 3: SEO Content Pillar (Days 61-90)
**Goal**: Organic discovery via niche keywords + competitive moat

### Week 9-10: Content Hub
Deploy **niche landing pages**:
- `/coaching/martial-arts` → BJJ + MMA specific drills + coach directory
- `/coaching/firearms` → Tactical training video feedback
- `/coaching/strength` → Strength coach directory + program templates
- `/coaching/crossfit` → CrossFit-specific progress tracking

Each page:
- Embedded coach showcases (filter by specialty)
- Testimonials + success metrics
- Schema.org BreadcrumbList + LocalBusiness markup
- Internal links to referral portal

### Week 11-12: Paid Amplification
- Google Ads: niche keywords ("best BJJ training app", "video form analysis", "online strength coaching")
- TikTok/Reels: ProgressCard viral clips (user-generated)
- Retargeting: Coach Showcase visitors → referral portal

**KPIs**:
- 2,000+ organic impressions per week
- 10% CTR on niche landing pages
- $0.50-$1.00 CAC (customer acquisition cost)
- 100+ coach signups, 2,000+ student invites
- 200-300 activated students across all channels

---

## Key Growth Levers

### Referral System
- **Activation trigger**: 3 completed drills (validate progress card share)
- **Reward tiers**: 1 friend = 1 week premium, 5 friends = lifetime access
- **Viral coefficient target**: K > 0.5 (sustainable growth)

### Coach Showcase
- Public-facing profile = lead magnet
- Leads flow to Coach contact form → email follow-up → paid coaching
- Enable coaches to earn revenue via student referrals

### ProgressCard Sharing
- Mobile-optimized card → easy download & social share
- Branded with Vellera logo → discovery
- Instagram Stories / TikTok native

### Content SEO
- Niche keywords (BJJ, MMA, tactical, strength coaching)
- Local SEO: "/" + coach directory for geo-targeted search
- Link juice: influencer sites → Vellera coach pages → referral portal
